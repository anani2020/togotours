Plantations

There are many types of plantations, such as: coffee, cacao, pineapple, palm trees, banana.

Butterflies

There are many butterflies native to Africa. Some of the species of Africa are: Papilionidae, Pieridae, Lycaenidae, Nymphalidae, and Hesperiidae.

Waterfalls

Africa is well known for its waterfalls. In Togo, there is the "Kpalime Falls" (Kpime Cascade). The waterfalls are more than 100 meters tall and among the tallest waterfalls in Togo.


Independence

There is a long history of Togo's independence. The "Monument de L'Independance is a tribute to Togo's Independence from France on April 27, 1960. The structure contains a human silhouette and is surrounded by palm trees, fountains, and a black and gold iron fence.

Animals

There are many animals that are well-known as native in Africa. The country of Togo has over 150 species of animals, such as Elephant, Cheetah, Lion, Gazelle, Antelope; and over 650 species of birds, such as Crane, Flamingo, Parrot, Pheasant, Quail.
