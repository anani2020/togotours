I was born in the city of Kpalime in the south of the country of Togo, which is located in West Africa. I worked as a tour guide in Kpalime and the capital city of Lome, having been self-employed from the age of 10 years. I met people from all over the world, while taking them on personalized tours in my country.

I have a high school diploma from Lycee Zomayi, Kpalime, Togo. I obtained a German language certificate from the Goethe Institute in Lome, Togo. I am fluent in English, French, German, and my country's Mina language and the Ewe dialect. I also attended the University of Togo Law School located in Lome, Togo.
